Maven Electronics Dashboard

Maven Electronics is at the forefront of innovation, providing high-quality electronic products to consumers worldwide. Our project aims to analyze sales data and customer behavior to enhance business strategies and improve customer satisfaction. By leveraging advanced data analytics and machine learning techniques, we aim to provide actionable insights that drive growth and efficiency.

This dataset provides a comprehensive view of sales, customer behavior, and product performance for a Maven Electronics retailer. It enables stakeholders to gain valuable insights into the retailer's operations through detailed analysis. Key performance indicators (KPIs) derived from this data are essential for understanding the business's overall performance.

The dataset includes historical sales data from various store locations worldwide, capturing transactions, product details, customer interactions, and financial aspects. It covers data from January 2016 to February 2021, offering a detailed look at the retailer's operations over this period. Below is a summary of the dataset's variables and descriptions.

Project Objective

  • Enhance Sales Insights: Provide detailed analysis of sales trends, seasonal patterns, and regional performance to optimize sales strategies.
  • Improve Customer Understanding: Analyze customer behavior and purchasing patterns to tailor marketing campaigns and improve customer engagement.
  • Support Strategic Decisions: Deliver data-driven insights to inform decision-making processes, ensuring strategic alignment with business goals.
  • Facilitate Accurate Reporting: Implement robust data modeling and ETL processes to ensure accurate and consistent reporting of sales data across various currencies.

Challenges

  • Data Quality and Consistency: Ensuring the accuracy and consistency of data collected from various sources.
  • Scalability: Developing models and systems that can handle large volumes of data and scale with business growth.
  • Currency Conversion: Managing a currency table with daily prices and calculating total sales accordingly. Data modeling was used to integrate this table successfully.
  • Integration: Integrating new analytical tools and models with existing business processes and systems.
  • Real-time Analysis: Providing real-time insights and analysis to support quick decision-making.

Methodology

  • Data Collection and Preprocessing: Sales data was extracted from SharePoint and imported into PowerBI for initial processing. This included cleaning, normalization, and merging of tables to ensure a cohesive dataset.
  • ETL Process: The Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) process was used to refine the data. Normalized tables were merged, and data modeling was performed to structure the data appropriately for analysis.
  • Data Modeling: Data modeling involved creating relationships between different tables, defining measures and calculated columns, and ensuring the data model supports the analytical requirements. A specific challenge was integrating a currency table with daily prices to accurately calculate total sales.
  • Visualization Development: Visuals were created in PowerBI according to stakeholder requirements, with a focus on conveying actionable business insights. These visuals included sales trends, customer segmentation, and performance metrics to provide a comprehensive view of the business.
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): Conducting EDA to uncover patterns and insights within the data, such as identifying high-performing products, seasonal trends, and regional sales distribution.
  • Machine Learning Models: Developing and deploying predictive models to forecast sales and customer behavior. Techniques include regression analysis, classification, and clustering to segment customers and predict future trends.
  • Interactive Dashboards: Using tools like Tableau and PowerBI to create interactive dashboards that present data in a clear and actionable manner. Dashboards include metrics like yearly, monthly, and weekly sales, sales per branch, and customer transaction analysis.

Analysis

  • Sales Channels: The online store contributed $11 million in sales, while offline sales amounted to $44 million, highlighting a notable difference in revenue generation between the two channels.
  • Seasonal Revenue Trends: Revenue exhibits clear seasonal patterns, with winter generating the highest revenue and spring recording the lowest, guiding strategic planning throughout the year.
  • Delivery Time Improvements: Over time, there has been a significant reduction in average delivery times, indicating enhanced operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.
  • Top Sales Performer: The United States emerges as the top sales-generating country, highlighting its importance as a key market for the company.
  • Product Category Overview: Sales breakdown reveals that the Computers category led with $19.3 million in revenue, followed by Home Appliances at $10.8 million, showcasing the diversity in product portfolio and consumer demand.
